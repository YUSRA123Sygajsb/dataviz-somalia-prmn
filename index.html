ABOUT PRMN
The PRMN (Protection & Return Monitoring Network) is a UNHCR-led project which identifies and reports on displacements (including returns) of populations in Somalia. The network also identifies and reports on protection risks and incidents underlying such movements. Only figures collected by PRMN are reported above. Not all displacements in Somalia are captured. This report should be read in conjunction with the Notes on PRMN Methodology (https://goo.gl/9uP6MJ) which outline the approach and certain limitations.
